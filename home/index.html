Hiiiii, I'm Duc Anh

And this is introducing me, literally :)))

Huhhhh tbh I don't really know what to write here — something big? Something cool? Something that captures the classic job interview question "Please describe yourself in 5 sentences" or something like that? But seriously, an individual is infinitely complex, arguably one of the most complex things in this universe — so how could anyone be captured in mere 5 sentences? What a reductive thing to ask. Or I guess to the capitalist, 5 sentences is enough to determine whether a person generates shareholder value or not?? But anyway, I'm too far off already, tho I could rant about this for a whole day lol :))


Indeed humans are vastly complex, but I'm not intimidated by it, and I'm fascinated by it, I guess? After all, once you know enough about the world to no longer be surprised by anything, humans become the endless object of learning and discovery. How fun!! Even after so many years of knowing myself, I'm still full of surprises from time to time :)))


So I guess what I'm trying to say is: I, as well as you — whoever stranger is reading these lines — am the universe's most complex creature. Trying to capture me in a few lines is doomed to be a futile attempt. Instead, let me show you WHAT I am. How does the vastly complex inner universe of me materialise in this world? Through my beliefs, my interests, my readings, my favourite movies and series, my personality (self-described), and most importantly, my worldview.


Tho it might be embarrassing, as some reel I saw on Instagram said: "It's fine to be embarrassing in pursuit of human connection" — so here goes nothing :)))

So yeah, you are welcomed!! Look around, discover who I am, and  you know what to do if you want to know more ...... :)))))))))
