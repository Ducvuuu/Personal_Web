About this place

Vu Trong Duc Anh

Policy work, fiction writing, kinda good taste in music (mostly it's just Vietnamese Indie music these days), and a perpetual love/hate relationship with my own inner life.

Welcome to my little corner on the internet, I like to think of it as my little home on the vast world web wide as well 🙂. Somewhere to keep the things that matter to me, to write when I feel like writing, and to share a little of who I am with people who are curious.

I'm Đức Anh. Vietnamese, 25 (maybe I should add a function to automatically update the age here lol), living in Jakarta. By day I work at the ASEAN Centre for Energy — policy research, data visualization, technical reports, so just meetings, report things. By night I'm writing fiction (once in a while :))), listening to Vietnamese Indie and doing nothing, wandering randomly on VR.... basically trying to make a "home" in a strange city that still doesn't feel like home after all this time.

Actually if we take a moment to think of it, to be able to get my own place on the Internet, to be able to customise it, literally make it anything I want anyhow, chaotic, playful, …… and accessible to all of humanity just by typing some random address on your phone, is kinda an amazing thing to do.

I think too deeply about most things. History, language, myth, traffic jam, sunlight piercing through the leaves, cats. I've mostly made peace with this. Mostly.

Come in, look around. Stay as long as you like.
