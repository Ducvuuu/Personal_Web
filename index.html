About this place

Vu Trong Duc Anh

Policy work, fiction writing, folk music, and a perpetual love/hate relationship with my own inner life.

This is my corner of the internet — less a portfolio, more a home. Somewhere to keep the things that matter to me, to write when I feel like writing, and to share a little of who I am with people who are curious.

I'm Anh. Vietnamese, 27, living in Jakarta. By day I work at the ASEAN Centre for Energy — policy research, data visualization, technical reports, all ten ASEAN countries in the mix. By night I'm writing fiction, listening to quan họ, losing hours to Hearts of Iron, or cooking phở in a city that still doesn't quite feel like home after all this time.

I think too deeply about most things. History, language, myth, what it means to carry a culture with you when you live somewhere else. I've mostly made peace with this. Mostly.

Come in, look around. Stay as long as you like.

Work

What I do from 9 to 6, and sometimes after

I work at the intersection of energy policy and communication — turning complex regional data into something people can actually use and understand.

Data Visualization

ASEAN Energy Visualizations

Choropleth maps, scatter plots, and interactive charts for APAEC and ALCBT publications. Making ten countries' worth of energy data legible — one chart at a time.

Policy Research

Building Energy Certification in ASEAN

A ten-country deep dive into green building systems — GREENSHIP, Green Mark, LOTUS, LEED, EDGE, and more. The kind of work that takes months and hopefully lands in the right hands.

Market Analysis

AC Market Study

Quantitative analysis of air conditioner markets across ASEAN, including custom data visualizations and reconstructed CSPF methodology. Surprisingly interesting once you're in it.

Life

The stuff that doesn't fit anywhere else

This is the part of the site I like best. The things that aren't on my CV but are probably more me than anything that is.

Vietnamese Folk Music

Quan họ, ca trù, chầu văn. The older, stranger, more beautiful forms. I grew up with these sounds and I don't think I'll ever fully leave them behind. I listen most when I'm furthest from home.

Grand Strategy Games

Hearts of Iron 4 and EU4 mostly. Managing ideology, alliances, and industrial capacity at continental scale is deeply satisfying in a way I've stopped trying to justify. One more turn.

Fiction Writing

Two projects. One finished and quietly terrifying to share. One perpetually in progress and secretly my favorite thing I'm working on. Writing is where my inner life goes when it has nowhere else to be.

Learning Piano

Started as an adult. Still a beginner. It is humbling in exactly the right way and I recommend it to anyone who needs to be reminded that patience is a skill.

Cooking Vietnamese Food

Phở from scratch. Bún bò Huế when I'm ambitious. It's half hobby, half homesickness management, fully the best part of a Sunday in Jakarta.
